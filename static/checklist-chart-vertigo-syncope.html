Medical Decision Making

I think arrhythmia is unlikely. ECG shows normal sinus rhythm with no interval abnormalities such as QT prolongation or WPW. There are no findings to suggest Brugada syndrome. Cardiac monitoring in the emergency department revealed no tachycardic or bradycardic dysrhythmia. Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy was considered but there are no clear historical elements pointing towards this -- EKG is not suggestive, QRS voltage is not extremely large and there are no suggestive Q-waves.
